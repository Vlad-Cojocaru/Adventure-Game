Class Select

To begin, please select a class Guardian. Each class is unique in its own way, with different health, odds of winning fights, appearances, and armor sets available for each one. The higher the aptitude, the easier it will be to complete the quests. The higher the health, the easier it will be to defeat Oryx in your final battle. Click each image to view the class description, and lock it in when you have made your decision.
