Rules/How to Play

Your job as the player is to:

  1. Pick a class that will change the gameplay later on throughout the game
  2. Locate the three items, the Upgrade Module, the Enhancement Core, and the Ascendant Shard
  3. Complete each of the three quests to acquire each of the three items
  4. Buy the sword, shield, and armor from the shop to be able to play the final battle
  5. Play the final battle and defeat the boss
